SpatialOS World Creation Building Blocks

About this page

This tool is designed to help kickstart brainstorming while creating your own world on SpatialOS.

It offers a few examples of 'worlds' that you could choose to create and allows you to put together building blocks of potential entities, components, and the relevant schema code that can serve as a springboard to get started on building your simulation.
